The Plan

As an adventurer at heart, I'm always looking for the next best life experience. This is by far going to be my craziest yet. Here's the catch... I have no idea where I will end up.

21 Days

For 21 days I will be globetrotting around the world. I will be staying in each city I end up in for 2-3 days.

Unknown Destinations

I won't know where I'm going until I walk up to the ticketing counter at the airport. My travel fate lies in the hands of the universe... and my fiance (who is pretty freakin' awesome for supporting this crazy idea of mine), I'll explain more later.

Follow Along

Follow along... I'll be posting daily blog posts with photos and videos.
